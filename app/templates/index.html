Welcome to NormanPD Incident Data Visualizer

This web application allows you to upload NormanPD incident reports in CSV or PDF format. It processes the data and provides various visualizations and analytics.

Project Workflow

Upload data

Upload incident reports in CSV or PDF format or directly the URLs.

Supported formats: CSV, PDF.

Ensure the files follow the specified format.

Process and Augment

Process URLs directly to fetch incident reports.

Features in original data: Date/Time, Incident Number, Location, Nature, Incident ORI.

Features in augmented data: Day of the Week, Time of Day, Weather, Location Rank, Side of Town, Incident Rank, Nature, EMSSTAT.

Visualizer

Visualize data with bar charts, line graphs, and pie charts.

Bar charts: Incident counts by nature.

Line graphs: Incident trends over time.

Pie charts: Distribution of incidents by nature.

Download

Download the augmented data.

Available formats: CSV, JSON.

Includes all processed and augmented features.
